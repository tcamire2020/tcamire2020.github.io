The Infield

Also known as the diamond, the infield is baseball's action-packed center. Full of line drives, pickles, double plays, bad hops, and diving stops, the infield is a stage for the highlight reel.

Six of the nine defensive positions in baseball are in the infield. Each with their own unique duty and many characteristics that make up an individual position player. Here is where you can learn the many differences and similarities between all of them. Just click on the link at each of the positions to learn all about them!
